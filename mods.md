### Origins
[curse Forge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/origins-forge)
> :warning: is temporarly removed because of a Mohist crash.

Origins is a mod that's sopposed to add races and classes to the modpack. I am using a custom datapack to add these:
#### Races
- Dwarf: enchant pickaxes, bonus pickaxe damage, better at mining, 1 block tall, coal diet :white_check_mark:
- Elf: bonus bow damage, bad mining, better in forest, vegetarian diet :white_check_mark:
- Troll: bonus axe damage, 3 blocks tall, meat diet, cyclops neutral
- Feline: 1.5 tall, no fall damage, 1.5 jump height :white_check_mark:
- Kitsune: fox morph (uses identity), intelligent (bonus with daggers, knives from Spartan Weaponry), charismatic (humanoids have a chance to not hit, and on hit take emotional damage)
#### Classes
- Hoarder: Bonus nonpersistent chest inventory
- Caster: Bonus Ars mana capacity and regen
- Explorer: Bonus movement speed, can climb walls
- Fighter: 10 bonus health; deals extra damage
#### Relations
Requires: Caelus API\
Addons: Expandabilaty; Apugli; Pehkui\
User dependency: Identity
